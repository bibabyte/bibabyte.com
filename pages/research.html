Why don't small businesses have websties?


  • Small businesses often do not focus their resources on technology. They are busy with all of the other tasks involved in running their business and lack the time to adopt new technology.
    - Digital Marketing and SMEs: A Systematic Mapping Study (2021)
    - OECD Digital for SMEs Global Initiative (2023)
    - Information and Communication Technologies Adoption: Evidence from Small Firms (2018)

  • A low rate of using technology can be attributed to:
    1. lack of knowledge about the usefulness and benefits of technology
    2. lack of knowledge and skills
    3. financial constraints
    - Information and Communication Technologies Adoption: Evidence from Small Firms (2018)
